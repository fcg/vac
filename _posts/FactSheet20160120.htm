New Zealand Residence Programme

Fortnightly Selection Statistics - 20 January 2016

1. Selection 20 January 2016– Skilled Migrant Category

The most recent selection of Expressions of Interest (EOIs) under the Skilled Migrant Category took place on 20 January 2016.

•585 EOIs were selected that had a points total of 140 or more;

•104 EOIs were selected that had a points total of 100 or more but less than 140, and included points for offers of skilled employment or current skilled employment in New Zealand.

As a result, 689 EOIs representing 1,426 people were selected and will now undergo preliminary verification to determine if an invitation to apply for residency will be issued.

There are 1,021 EOIs in the pool after the selection.
